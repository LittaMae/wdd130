Overview

Purpose

The purpose for the site is to use technology to advertise whitewater rafting excursions and sale trips to those interested. It will contain information about the company and about the trips that are offered.

Audience

Who is my audience? - Active individuals interested in whitewater rafting.

What do they expect to find when accessing the site? - All information of trips and offerings, prices, and a way to reserve and pay.

What will be used to access the site? PC's, laptops, tablets, and phones.
